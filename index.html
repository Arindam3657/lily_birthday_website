BIRTHDAY

Dear lily,

A special day, your journey takes.
In whispers soft, the morning breaks,
Beneath the gentle dawn's embrace,
Your radiance shines, a tender grace.

On this day, as the world awakes,
Let laughter dance, let joy partake.
For in your eyes, a spark ignites,
A flame of love that forever alights.

Each breath you take, a melody sweet,
In every step, a rhythm complete.
Today, my dear, let your heart soar,
As dreams unfold and spirits roar.

Happy birthday, lovely one divine,
In your presence, love's stars align.
May this day, with its gentle hue,
Bring endless bliss and love anew.

So blow the candles, make a wish true,
In every heartbeat, I'm here for you.
For you're the melody in my song,
Forever cherished, forever strong.
